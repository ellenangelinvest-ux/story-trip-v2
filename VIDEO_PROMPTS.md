# StoryTrip - Combined 30-Second Video Prompt for Google Veo 3

## Main Prompt (Detailed):
```
A cinematic 30-second promotional video for "StoryTrip" travel app showcasing 3 key features.

Scene 1 - NARRATIVE TRIPS (0-10s):
Opens with golden sunrise over mountains. Phone screen shows beautiful app interface with story categories "Underdog Redemption", "Solo Discovery", "Family Legacy" in warm teal and orange colors. User taps a category, destinations appear - Bali temples, Tokyo neon streets, Santorini sunset. Text overlay: "Choose Your Story"

Scene 2 - SQUAD MATCHING (10-20s):
Animated profile cards with personality types (ENFP, INTJ) and photos float and magnetically connect together, showing 94% compatibility. Quick cut to diverse group of 4 travelers (mixed ethnicities, 20s-30s) meeting at airport with excited hugs, then montage: laughing on boat, group selfie at landmark, sharing street food. Text overlay: "Find Your Tribe"

Scene 3 - YOUR FILM (20-30s):
Travel photos arrange on cinematic timeline, transform into letterbox film with Ken Burns effect. Chapter title "The Journey" appears like movie credits. Quick cuts of dramatic moments - silhouette against sunset, summit triumph, emotional reflection. Ends with movie poster featuring traveler as star. Final text: "StoryTrip - Your Story. Your Journey. Your Film." Logo reveal with warm glow.

Style: Cinematic, warm golden tones, film grain, 24fps, inspirational music implied through editing rhythm. Premium travel brand aesthetic.
```

## How to Use:

1. Go to **Google AI Studio**: https://aistudio.google.com/
2. Select **Veo 3** model
3. Paste the prompt above
4. Set duration: **30 seconds**
5. Set aspect ratio: **16:9** (landscape) or **9:16** (mobile/TikTok)
6. Generate and download
